The primary census report of 2011, gives the number of ethnic groups of bangladesh as 27. Comparatively, Chakma are the largest and Marma are the second largest ethnic groups of Bangladesh.




Chakma

Areas of Living: Rangamati, Bandarban, Khagrachari
Origin: Southwestern Burma
Dialect: Arakanese Indo-European Tongue
Traditional Wear: Pinon, Khadi(Woman),Dhuti(man)
Religion: Buddhism
Main Festival: Biju
Cultivation Method: Jhum

Areas of Living: Rangamati, Bandarban, Khagrachari, Cox's Bazar, Ptuakhali
